About ESSCA

ESSCA (École Supérieure des Sciences Commerciales d'Angers) is one of the top business schools in Europe, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and executive education programs. Established in 1909, ESSCA combines tradition and innovation to deliver world-class learning experiences.

Our programs emphasize practical learning, international exposure, and leadership development, preparing students to excel in a rapidly changing business landscape. With a strong network of corporate partners and alumni, ESSCA connects education with professional success.
